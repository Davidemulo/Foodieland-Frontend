How do you start in the food industry?

Many chefs begin in entry-level kitchen roles, learning food prep and operations. Becoming a professional chef requires dedication, creativity, and discipline. It’s more than cooking — it’s about mastering techniques and creating experiences.

What do you love most about your job?

Creating meaningful experiences through food is deeply fulfilling.

Do you cook at home?

Most chefs prefer simple meals at home, focusing on comfort over complexity.
